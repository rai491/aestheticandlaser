5 Things to Consider Before Choosing a Body Contouring Treatment

05 Sep, 2023
At The Center for Laser and Aesthetic Medicine , we understand that deciding to undergo body contouring treatment is a big decision. That’s why it’s important to take the time to consider all options before choosing a treatment. Here are five things you should keep in mind when evaluating your options. 1. Safety: Safety should always be the number one priority when considering any medical procedure, and body contouring treatments are no exception. Do your research to ensure that the clinic you choose is properly certified and uses only FDA-approved technology for optimal safety. 2. Cost: Body contouring treatments can sometimes cost thousands of dollars or more, so it’s important to weigh potential up-front costs against potential long-term benefits such as improved confidence and self-esteem. Make sure you understand all the associated costs, including those related to post-treatment care and maintenance, before making your final decision. 3. Techniques: There are many different techniques for body contouring, ranging from laser liposuction to ultrasonic cavitation. Research each technique carefully to determine which one is best suited for your individual needs and goals before committing to a treatment plan. 4. Procedure Time: Be aware that body contouring treatments require some amount of recovery time, depending on the specific technique involved and the extent of treatment needed. Make sure your schedule allows enough time for proper healing and follow-up appointments before going ahead with a procedure. 5. Expertise: It goes without saying that the doctor performing any type of medical procedure should have experience working with patients like yourself who are seeking body contouring treatments specifically. Find out how many years of experience they have in their field as well as what other similar procedures they have performed in order to get an idea about both qualifications and results produced by a said doctor prior to committing to any particular course of action. Of note, our Sculpsure has no downtime, takes 25 minutes, and requires no post-op care. The Basics of Chemical Peels: Types, Benefits, and Considerations

08 Aug, 2023
Chemical peels have become an increasingly popular way to improve the look and feel of skin. Chemical peels are a type of exfoliation procedure that uses a chemical solution to remove dead skin cells and reveal new, smoother, younger-looking skin underneath. This cosmetic procedure helps to reduce wrinkles, acne scars, age spots, sun damage, uneven pigmentation, and other skin concerns. Chemical peels can also help even out your skin tone and texture for a more youthful complexion overall. At The Center for Laser and Aesthetic Medicine , we offer different types of chemical peels based on your individual needs. A light chemical peel (also known as a lunchtime or superficial peel) is great for those who want mild results with no downtime. Medium-depth or 20% gloycolic chemical peels are more aggressive treatments and can provide better results in reducing certain skin concerns such as discoloration or premature aging symptoms like wrinkles. Finally, deep chemical peels or 30% glycolic penetrate deeper layers of the skin which can lead to dramatic improvements in your overall appearance but will include some downtime afterwards. Chemical peels at The Center for Laser and Aesthetic Medicine also come with many benefits; not only do they reduce visible signs of aging such as fine lines, wrinkles, sagging and dullness but they also help brighten the complexion by improving discoloration from sun damage and fading dark spots for an even skin tone. Additionally, chemical peels help to clear up blemishes such as cystic acne scars which result in smoother-looking skin with fewer breakouts over time. Plus they are much less invasive than surgery or other extreme measures yet highly effective when done correctly! When considering any kind of cosmetic procedure it’s important to first consult with a skilled professional who can assess your individual needs and advise you on the most appropriate treatment plan. At The Center of Laser & Aesthetic Medicine, you can trust that you will receive personalized attention from our experienced aesthetician who will suggest the optimal course of action based on your goals for an improved complexion. Moreover, all our procedures come with detailed aftercare instructions which should be followed carefully in order to ensure successful results without any adverse reactions or complications down the line! Whether you are seeking subtle changes or dramatic improvements in the look and feel of your skin – chemical peels may be just what you need! The Benefits of Microneedling for Improving Skin Texture and Tone

05 Jul, 2023
The Center for Laser and Aesthetic Medicine is proud to present information about the growing trend of microneedling treatments for improving skin texture and tone. This innovative form of skincare has been shown to reduce wrinkles, improve complexion, and give a fresh, glowing look to the skin. Here is an overview of the many benefits of microneedling that have made it such a popular choice among skincare enthusiasts. Microneedling stimulates collagen production, which helps to increase firmness in the skin and reduce wrinkles. When used regularly, it can help even out fine lines and create a smoother surface on the face. Additionally, it encourages new cell turnover by creating microscopic channels in the skin that allow serums with beneficial ingredients like Vitamin C to penetrate deeply, resulting in a brighter overall tone. One of the most touted benefits of microneedling is increased hydration levels in the skin. The tiny wounds created during treatment prompt your body’s natural healing response to kick in; as part of this process, hydrating fluids are sent down from above in order to repair the area. This means that you will get a boost of moisture along with all of your other treatments! In addition to improved appearance and texture, microneedling can help your other products work more effectively too! The micro channels created from needling leave pores open for up to 24 hours following treatment so that topical products can be absorbed deeper than they would have been able to before—making them much more effective than they would have been otherwise! Finally, microneedling can be used on virtually any area of your body (including those hard-to-reach places) providing some incredible results! From full face treatments to specific areas like around the eyes or chin microneedling can help tighten sagging skin or reduce puffiness quickly and easily. How Laser Vein Treatment Can Improve the Appearance of Spider Veins

06 Jun, 2023
Spider veins, often seen as visible networks of red and blue discoloration on the skin’s surface, are a common condition that can be caused by aging, sun exposure, genetics, or other factors. For those wishing to aesthetically remove spider veins, laser vein treatment is available as a safe and effective alternative for reducing the appearance of these vessels. Laser vein treatment is an outpatient procedure that uses high-intensity light to target and heats the spider veins. This causes the vessel wall to collapse and seal shut permanently, resulting in a visible reduction in the size and intensity of the vessels over time. Generally speaking, patients may experience slight discomfort during this procedure, however, there is no need to use anesthesia or take any medications prior to or after treatment. Most people require only one laser vein session to see full results while others may need multiple procedures depending on their individual needs. The number of treatments required will also depend on each person’s individual condition but typically takes around 3–6 months before fully resolving itself. In addition to its ease and speed of recovery times, laser vein treatments offer many other benefits such as the minimal risk of side effects since most patients do not experience any pain or bruising post-treatment; no downtime needed so it does not interfere with work schedules or daily activities; long term results - because once destroyed vessels cannot reform themselves; and improved safety due to less chance of infection. Our medial provider could recommend some post care instructions such as curtailing rigorous exercising for a reasonable amount of time. Overall, laser vein therapy offers those suffering from unsightly spider veins an excellent option for reducing their visibility without having to undergo more invasive techniques like surgery. The Difference Between Laser Hair Removal and Electrolysis

04 May, 2023
Deciding between laser hair removal and electrolysis for your unwanted body hair can be a difficult choice. While both treatments are effective, they work in very different ways and it’s important to understand the differences before deciding which is best for you. Laser hair removal works by aiming a beam of light at the pigment in the hair follicle, damaging it and preventing the growth of future hairs. Laser hair reduction is most effective with dark, course hair as the light energy is absorbed by the melanin in the folliles. It is fast, relatively painless and can treat large areas of skin all at once. However, some people may need multiple treatments over time to achieve desired results and there is no guarantee that every single follicle will be destroyed. Electrolysis on the other hand uses shortwave radio frequencies to destroy individual hairs at their root with small needles. This method has higher accuracy than laser hair removal as each follicle can be targeted separately and completely destroyed if needed because each individual follicle needs to be treated separately, sessions are much longer than laser treatments and require more visits over an extended period of time to achieve desired results – making it more costly in the long run. Electrolysis is good for all hair types. Ultimately, both laser hair removal and electrolysis are viable options for dealing with unwanted body hair but depending on individual circumstances such as cost effectiveness or skin type one may be more suitable than the other. Consulting a skilled aestheticia n or dermatologist can help you decide which treatment would be best for you based on your unique needs and goals!

Non-surgical Options for Facial Rejuvenation

05 Apr, 2023
As we age, it is common to experience some degree of facial imperfections such as sun damage, wrinkles and sagging. While surgery can be an effective way to restore your youthful appearance, non-surgical options are becoming increasingly popular due to their less invasive nature and quicker recovery times. Here are a few non-surgical treatments that can help rejuvenate your face without the need for surgery. Dermal fillers are injectable substances designed to fill in lines and creases in the skin caused by aging. Commonly used for wrinkles around the nose and mouth, dermal fillers can also be used to add volume and enhance specific facial features such as cheeks or lips. Botox injections involve injecting a small amount of botulinum toxin into specific areas on the face to relax muscles that cause wrinkles. Botox injections work best on frown lines, crows feet, and forehead wrinkles. Laser resurfacing is a non-invasive procedure used to improve the texture and tone of the skin using laser light energy or pulsed light. This treatment is most commonly used for removing fine lines and reducing acne scarring by stimulating collagen production in the targeted area. It can also be used to remove unwanted hair or pigmentation irregularities caused by melasma or sun damage. Microdermabrasion uses tiny crystals sprayed onto the skin’s surface which then buffs away dryness, blemishes, and discoloration resulting from sun damage or acne scars. Microdermabrasion is best suited for those looking to achieve smooth skin without undergoing chemical peels or laser resurfacing treatments which are often more aggressive methods of exfoliation. There is also Thread Lifting which produces a visible lift with a minimally invasive procedure by inserting biodegradable absorbable threads called PDO (Polydioxanone) sutures to lift and tighten the skin and induce collagen. Non-invasive alternatives such as these offer a safe and highly effective solution when it comes to facial rejuvenation without needing surgery or downtime periods associated with traditional procedures making them an attractive option for those looking for anti-aging solutions without going under the knife!

Tips for Maintaining Healthy, Youthful Skin As You Age

06 Mar, 2023
As you get older, wrinkles and age spots can start to appear on your skin. With the right combination of lifestyle changes, skincare products and aesthetic treatments , you can keep your skin looking healthy and youthful over time. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, youthful skin as you age: 1. Wear sunscreen - To protect your face from UV damage, wear a broad-spectrum sunscreen with SPF 30 or higher every day. Make sure to reapply periodically throughout the day to ensure full coverage. 2. Follow a consistent skincare regimen - Use a mild cleanser and gentle exfoliant two to three times per week, followed by a lightweight moisturizer with peptides and antioxidants. Make sure to apply eye cream around the eye area to reduce signs of aging such as wrinkles and under-eye circles. And don't forget to use an anti-aging serum at nighttime! 3. Eat a balanced diet - Eating a balanced diet rich in healthy fats and proteins will help keep your skin looking young and glowing from the inside out. Include plenty of fresh fruits and vegetables as well as lean proteins like fish, nuts, eggs and legumes in your meals every day for maximum benefits. 4. Get enough sleep - Sleep is essential for maintaining healthy skin in the long term, so aim for 7-9 hours each night. While you're sleeping, your body repairs itself from any damage it sustained during the day – so make sure you're getting enough quality rest time for optimum results! 5. Aesthetic treatments – Aesthetic treatments such as laser therapy or chemical peels can help reduce wrinkles or blemishes while stimulating collagen production underneath the surface of the skin. However, these treatments should always be done by a trained physician and esthetician who has experience performing these procedures safely and effectively. By following these tips each day and visiting The Center for Laser and Aesthetic Medicine regularly, will guarantee that your skin remains soft supple vibrant & youthful even well into old age!
